FLAIR-GG Privacy Policy Effective Date: 2023-12-01

1. Introduction


Welcome to FLAIR-GG. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information about you when you use our services. By using FLAIR-GG, you consent to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect>


Usage Data: We collect data on how you use our services, including website URLs, query results, and public metadata from third-party sites. Public Data: All data collected has been explicitly made public by the resource providers.

3. How We Collect Data


We collect data through: Metadata Harvesting Engines: Automated tools to gather public metadata. Web Forms: Where users might voluntarily provide information.

4. Use of Collected Information


Primary Use: To simplify federated access to multiple resources simultaneously and provide data for custom analytical tools. Secondary Use: To increase visibility for network participants, driving traffic to their websites.

5. Sharing of Data


We do not share data with specific third parties. Instead, we republish data that has already been made public by resource providers.

6. Data Security


Since the data we handle is already public, we do not implement specific security measures beyond standard web practices.

7. Your Rights


Withdrawal: You have the right to withdraw your data at any time without needing assistance from the project.

8. Data Retention


Retention Period: Data is typically retained for 1-2 weeks, with automated refreshes at the end of this period. Custom Retention: Network participants can set their own retention times, which are respected by the network.

9. International Data Transfers


Our services allow access to data from any country via the web.

10. Cookies


We do not currently use cookies or similar technologies.

11. Children's Privacy


FLAIR-GG does not knowingly collect any data from children under the age of 13.
